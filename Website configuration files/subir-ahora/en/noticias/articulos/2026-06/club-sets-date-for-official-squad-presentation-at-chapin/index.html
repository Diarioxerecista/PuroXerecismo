Club sets date for official squad presentation at Chapín

Supporters will get their first look at the complete squad ahead of the first pre-season friendly.

Xerez CD are preparing the official presentation event for the 2026/27 squad, where fans will see all the summer's new faces alongside the new coaching staff for the first time.

The club has not yet confirmed the exact date, though the event is expected to take place in the coming weeks, ahead of the first pre-season friendly.
