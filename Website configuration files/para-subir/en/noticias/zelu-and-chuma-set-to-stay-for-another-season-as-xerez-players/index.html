Zelu and Chuma set to stay for another season as Xerez players

Xerez CD continue shaping their squad for 2026/27 with the continuity of two familiar faces in the division: winger Zelu and forward Chuma.

Xerez CD continue putting together the squad that will play under Xavi Molist in the 2026/27 season. In addition to the already confirmed renewal of Guille Campos, the club is close to finalising the continuity of both Zelu and Chuma, who will remain for another season as Xerez players.

Zelu, one of the few players who already had a contract in place at Xerez CD, thus extends his stay with the blue-and-white side, having become a regular presence in attack over recent seasons.

Meanwhile, Rafael González Rodríguez, 'Chuma', a Seville native, will embark on his second consecutive season as a blue-and-white after returning to the club last January, in his second spell as a Xerez player, arriving from Poland's K.S. Wieczysta Kraków. Coming through the Sevilla FC academy, the forward has built a wide-ranging career with clubs including UB Lebrijana, Córdoba CF, San Roque de Lepe, UCAM Murcia and Mérida AD.

Both renewals add to the moves already confirmed this summer, in a transfer window in which the club is also working to officially confirm the arrival of Fali Giménez in the coming days.
