Xerez CD sign U23 winger Jaime Fernández

Foto: Xerez CD

The Jerez-born player, on loan this past season from Villarreal at Yeclano Deportivo, strengthens the right flank of the blue-and-white attack in an U23 squad slot.

Xerez CD have made official the signing of Jaime Fernández Higuero (Jerez de la Frontera, 29 October 2005) for the 2026/27 season. The right winger, who can also play as an inside forward, brings pace, dribbling and directness to the attack, occupying an U23 squad slot.

The Jerez native played this season at Yeclano Deportivo on loan from Villarreal, though without much playing time in Segunda Federación: 12 appearances without a single start, just over 200 minutes and one goal, scored at the Nuevo Colombino against Recreativo de Huelva.

Before his spell in Villarreal's youth system, Fernández came through the Xerez DFC academy after spells at Atlético Sanluqueño and a season at Guadalcacín, going on to feature in a handful of first-team games in the 23/24 season.
