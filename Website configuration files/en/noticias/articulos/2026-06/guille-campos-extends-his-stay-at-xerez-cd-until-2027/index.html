Guille Campos extends his stay at Xerez CD until 2027

Foto: PuroXerecismo

The defender from Sanlúcar de Barrameda continues for another season after being a key figure in the division's best defensive record.

Xerez CD have confirmed the renewal of Guille Campos until the end of the 2026/27 season. The Sanlúcar-born defender played 31 matches last season, 30 of them as a starter, racking up over 2,500 minutes and one goal.

His consistency and versatility to play in different positions across the back line were, according to the club, key factors in Xerez CD finishing last season as the league's least-breached side.

With this renewal, the coaching staff retain one of the most stable pieces of the defensive block ahead of the new project led by Xavi Molist.
