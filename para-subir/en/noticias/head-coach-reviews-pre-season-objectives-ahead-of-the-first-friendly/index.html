Press conference

Head coach reviews pre-season objectives ahead of the first friendly

Xavi Molist faced the media for the first time this summer, looking back on the opening weeks of work and previewing the keys to the season ahead.

"We're in a process of adaptation, both physically and tactically. We need time for the team to fully understand what we want", explained the Catalan coach in his first appearance of the summer.

On the transfer market, he was cautious: "We're still working with the club to finish putting the squad together. We want to reach September with a complete team."
