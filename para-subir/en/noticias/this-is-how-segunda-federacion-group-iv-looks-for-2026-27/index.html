This is how Segunda Federación Group IV looks for 2026/27

A look at the 18 teams that will make up the group this season, featuring sides from Andalusia, the Canary Islands and Extremadura.

Group IV of Segunda Federación will this season bring together historic Andalusian football clubs alongside reserve teams from La Liga and Segunda División, as well as representatives from the Canary Islands and Extremadura.

The competition format remains unchanged from previous seasons: a double round-robin league, with direct promotion for the champions and a promotion play-off for the clubs finishing between second and fifth.
