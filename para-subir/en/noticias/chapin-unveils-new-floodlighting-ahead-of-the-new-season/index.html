Chapín unveils new floodlighting ahead of the new season

The municipal stadium renews its lighting system, improving visibility for both players and broadcasters.

The installation, jointly funded by the club and the local council, replaces the old floodlight system with state-of-the-art LED technology.

The upgrade arrives in time for the start of the new season, during which several matches could be played under lights for broadcasting reasons.

The club also points to the energy savings the renovation will bring in the medium term.
