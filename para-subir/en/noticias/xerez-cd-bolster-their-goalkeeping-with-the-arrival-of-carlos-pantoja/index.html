Xerez CD bolster their goalkeeping with the arrival of Carlos Pantoja

The Madrid-born goalkeeper signs for the 2026/27 season after a campaign of complete trust at RSD Alcalá in Segunda Federación.

Xerez CD have officially confirmed the signing of Carlos Pantoja Valadés, a goalkeeper born in Pozuelo de Alarcón (Madrid) in 2000, who arrives to take over between the posts following the departure of Ángel de la Calzada, last season's Group Zamora Trophy winner.

Pantoja arrives with a solid track record: he started all 34 league matches for RSD Alcalá, playing over 3,000 minutes and conceding only 39 goals, a tally that made his side one of the league's least breached defences. Before his spell at Alcalá he played for clubs such as Rayo Vallecano B, Complutense Alcalá and CF Collado Villalba, gaining experience in both Segunda and Tercera Federación.

The club highlights his command of his area, his strength in the air and his leadership from the back, qualities that head coach Xavi Molist's staff consider key to the project for next season in Group IV.
