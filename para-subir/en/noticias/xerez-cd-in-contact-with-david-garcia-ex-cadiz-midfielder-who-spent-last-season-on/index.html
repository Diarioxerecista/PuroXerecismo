Xerez CD in contact with David García, ex-Cádiz midfielder who spent last season on loan at Ibiza

Foto: PuroXerecismo

The Jerez club have opened talks with the midfielder, who spent last season on loan at UD Ibiza from Cádiz CF.

Xerez CD have made contact with David García, a midfielder who arrived at Cádiz CF and spent last season on loan at UD Ibiza, this newspaper can confirm. The player is now a free agent following the end of his contract with the Cádiz club, and is seen as a realistic option to strengthen the Xerez midfield.

García is a midfielder with experience at professional and semi-professional level, comfortable combining in tight spaces and with the engine to cover ground. His profile would suit the system Xavi Molist wants to implement for the coming season.

Talks are at an early stage and no agreement has been reached yet. The sporting department continues to work on finalising the squad ahead of the start of pre-season on 20 July.
