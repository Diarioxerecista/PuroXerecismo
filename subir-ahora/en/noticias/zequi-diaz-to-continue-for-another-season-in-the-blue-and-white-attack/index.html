Zequi Díaz to continue for another season in the blue-and-white attack

Foto: PuroXerecismo

The winger from Sanlúcar de Barrameda, who joined as a winter reinforcement last season, remains under contract for 2026/27.

Ezequiel Díaz Romero, known as 'Zequi Díaz', will remain a Xerez CD player for another season. The right-footed winger, who joined the club in last season's winter transfer window, played 16 matches, 12 of them as a starter, scoring two goals.

The club particularly values his dribbling ability, individual skill and the danger he creates from the flank, qualities the coaching staff want to keep making the most of in attack next season.

His continued stay adds to that of other players already confirmed, in a squad Xerez CD continue to shape ahead of the start of the competition.
