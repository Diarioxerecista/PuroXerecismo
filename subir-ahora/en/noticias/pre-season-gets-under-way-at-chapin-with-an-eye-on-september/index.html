Pre-season gets under way at Chapín with an eye on September

Almost the entire squad has begun training under Xavi Molist, with the league start date still to be confirmed.

Xerez CD have started the 2026/27 pre-season with most of the squad already available to the coaching staff. The opening sessions have focused on physical conditioning following the summer break.

Head coach Xavi Molist, who is taking charge of his first full season at the helm of the blue-and-whites, will have several weeks of work before the campaign's first official fixture.

The club has not yet finalised the pre-season friendly schedule, although the first warm-up opponents are expected to be confirmed in the coming days.
