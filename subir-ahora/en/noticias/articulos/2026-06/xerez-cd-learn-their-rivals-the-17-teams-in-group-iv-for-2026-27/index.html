Xerez CD learn their rivals: the 17 teams in Group IV for 2026/27

The Spanish Football Federation confirms the make-up of the group for next season, featuring clubs from Andalusia, the Canary Islands and Extremadura. The official fixture list will be announced in the coming weeks.

The Royal Spanish Football Federation has finalised the structure of the Segunda Federación groups for the 2026/27 season. Xerez CD will once again compete in Group IV, alongside clubs from Andalusia, the Canary Islands and Extremadura.

Confirmed opponents include sides such as Atlético Sanluqueño, Marbella, Linares Deportivo, Recreativo de Huelva and Sevilla Atlético, as well as Canarian reserve teams Las Palmas Atlético, Tenerife B and Tamaraceite, plus Extremaduran clubs Badajoz and Don Benito.

The club approaches the new campaign aiming to fight for promotion to Primera Federación, after last season ended in the first round of the promotion play-offs against Conquense.

A detailed match-by-match calendar has yet to be confirmed; the Federation is expected to publish it in the coming weeks, with the regular season set to kick off in September.
