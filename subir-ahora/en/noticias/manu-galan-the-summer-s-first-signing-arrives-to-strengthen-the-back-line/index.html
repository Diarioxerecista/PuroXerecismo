Manu Galán, the summer's first signing, arrives to strengthen the back line

The Sevillian centre-back joins from Deportiva Minera, the Murcian club that fell just short of promotion to Primera Federación.

Manuel Galán Ubero, known as 'Manu Galán', a Seville native, becomes Xerez CD's first confirmed signing for the 2026/27 season. The centre-back arrives after a season with Deportiva Minera, the Murcian club that came close to direct promotion in Group IV.

Coming through the Sevilla FC academy, Galán has played for a number of clubs on his way to Jerez: Utrera, Cabecense, Azuaga and Huesca B in Tercera, before joining Recreativo de Huelva, with whom he achieved two consecutive promotions, and a spell at Melilla before his most recent stint at Minera.

The club is relying on his experience and leadership qualities to add solidity to a defence that head coach Xavi Molist is putting the finishing touches to ahead of the league opener in September.
