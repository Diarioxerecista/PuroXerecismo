Pre-season

Xerez CD confirm start of training and their first friendly match

Foto: Xerez CD

The club has announced that the 2026/27 pre-season will begin on 20 July, with the first warm-up match set for 1 August against Jerez Industrial.

Xerez CD have confirmed the first dates of the 2026/27 pre-season. The squad will begin training on 20 July, kicking off physical and technical preparation ahead of the new campaign.

The first friendly of the summer will come on 1 August, against Jerez Industrial, a fixture that has become a regular pre-season opener in recent years.

The club has not yet confirmed the rest of the friendly schedule, though further pre-season opponents are expected to be announced in the coming weeks ahead of the start of the official competition in September.
