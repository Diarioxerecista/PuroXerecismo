Ismael Gutiérrez stays for another year in the Xerez midfield

Foto: PuroXerecismo

The midfielder, last season's MVP, extends his stay with the club after being a key player for the team last season.

Xerez CD have confirmed the continuity of Ismael Gutiérrez Montilla, who will play his second season in Jerez after a campaign in which he was a key player for the team, scoring two goals.

The midfielder ended last season as the 2025/26 MVP, a recognition the club attributes to his composure on the ball, his ability to balance the team, and the leadership he showed in midfield.

His renewal adds continuity to one of the key positions in Xavi Molist's system, who already counts on one of his most decisive players for the 2026/27 project.
