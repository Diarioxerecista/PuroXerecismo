Club continues working the transfer market ahead of the league start

The sporting management has not ruled out further incomings before the summer transfer window closes.

In addition to the moves already confirmed, Xerez CD continue working the transfer market with the aim of having the squad finalised before the start of the official competition.

The club is urging caution until each deal is fully completed, while acknowledging the market is expected to keep moving in the coming weeks.
