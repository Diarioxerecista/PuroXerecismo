Xerez CD seal the signing of Rubén Mesa, Deportiva Minera's top scorer

Foto: Xerez CD

The blue-and-white side strengthen their attack with a forward from Extremadura who scored thirteen goals last season between Estepona and Deportiva Minera.

Xerez CD have officially confirmed the arrival of Rubén Mesa for the 2026/27 season. The forward joins from Deportiva Minera, the Cartagena-based club with whom he played the second half of last season after leaving Estepona in the winter transfer window.

The Extremaduran striker ended last season as one of the most in-form forwards in Group IV: seven goals with Minera and six with Estepona, finishing as the Cartagena side's top scorer in the closing stretch of the campaign.

His arrival adds to the moves the sporting management has been closing this summer, as head coach Xavi Molist puts the finishing touches to his squad ahead of the league opener in September.
