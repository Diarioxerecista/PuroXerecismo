Bittor Isuskiza, new Xerez CD player

Foto: PuroXerecismo

As announced a few days ago, the Basque midfielder becomes a new blue-and-white player for the 2026/27 season.

As announced a few days ago, Bittor Isuskiza becomes a new Xerez CD player. The Basque midfielder, born in Güeñes (Biscay) in 1998, joins as a free agent after his previous contract expired on 1 July.

Isuskiza came through the Barakaldo CF academy, a club where he passed the century mark of official appearances across several spells, and has also played for Sestao River and Portugalete. Over his career he has achieved three promotions to Segunda Federación, experience Xerez CD particularly value given the challenge of competing in that division next season.

With this addition, Isuskiza joins already-announced arrivals Carlos Pantoja, Manu Galán, Fali, Rubén Mesa, Jaime Fernández and Víctor López in the squad being assembled for the 2026/27 season.
