Interview · Dressing room

"We're arriving hungry to fight for the top spots from day one", admits the captain

The team captain looks back on the start of pre-season, the arrival of new faces, and the goals for the campaign ahead.

Speaking informally after one of the first pre-season sessions, the Xerez captain expressed enthusiasm about the start of the new project: "You can feel a different atmosphere from day one, there's real eagerness to start competing as soon as possible."

On the summer signings, he highlighted the quality they bring to the dressing room: "The lads who have joined know the division well, and you can feel that in the day-to-day training."

Asked about the season's objective, he didn't hold back: "Last year we fell just short. This year we want to fight for the top from the very start."
