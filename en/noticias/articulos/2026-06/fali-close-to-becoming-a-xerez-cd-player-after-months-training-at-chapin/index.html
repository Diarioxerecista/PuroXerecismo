Fali close to becoming a Xerez CD player after months training at Chapín

The Valencian centre-back, formerly of Cádiz CF, has been training with the blue-and-whites since February and is reportedly close to signing as a new addition to the back line.

Fali Giménez, a 32-year-old defender from Valencia, is reportedly very close to becoming a new Xerez CD player for the 2026/27 season, according to several local media outlets. The club has not yet confirmed the move officially.

The centre-back began training with the blue-and-white side last February, after leaving Cádiz CF, while deciding his professional future. Coming through the Villarreal and Levante academies, he has eight seasons as a Cádiz player under his belt, four of them in Primera División, as well as spells at Huracán de Valencia, Nástic de Tarragona and Barcelona B.

His profile, capable of playing both as a centre-back and a defensive midfielder, would make for a reliable addition to a back line head coach Xavi Molist is still putting together ahead of the start of the competition.
