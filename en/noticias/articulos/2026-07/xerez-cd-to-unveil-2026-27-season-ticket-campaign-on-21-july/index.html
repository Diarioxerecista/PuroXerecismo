Xerez CD to unveil 2026/27 season-ticket campaign on 21 July

The club has set the date and time for the presentation of its 2026/27 season-ticket campaign, though further details have yet to be revealed.

Xerez CD have confirmed that they will present their season-ticket campaign for the 2026/27 season on 21 July, at 20:00.

The club has not yet released further information about the event, such as the venue or season-ticket prices and categories. These details are expected to be confirmed in the coming days.
