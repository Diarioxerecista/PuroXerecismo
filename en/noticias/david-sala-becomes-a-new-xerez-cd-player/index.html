David Sala, new blue-and-white signing pending official confirmation

The agreement between Xerez CD and the right-back is essentially done; only the formality of the official announcement remains, expected in the coming days.

Jerez de la Frontera, 10 July 2026. Xerez Club Deportivo continue shaping their squad for the 2026/2027 season, the blue-and-white side's third in a row in Segunda RFEF, and everything points to David Sala being the next piece to arrive. The right-back joins from Albacete B, the reserve side that occupies the U23 slot in its Segunda Federación group. This outlet has been able to confirm, through sources close to the negotiation, that the agreement between the club and the player is essentially closed and only the formality of making it official remains, expected in the coming days.

Neither Xerez CD nor the player's camp have wished to comment publicly yet, in line with the discretion both parties usually keep until the formal announcement. However, the same sources maintain that the contract terms are already agreed and no setbacks are expected that would put the deal at risk.

A move within the summer planning

Sala's arrival fits into the roadmap the sporting department has drawn up for this summer market, in which the signings of Carlos Pantoja, Manu Galán, Fali and Rubén Mesa have already been completed. The club is looking to complete a competitive squad for a season that looks especially demanding, with the new addition of trips to the Canary Islands introduced by the new configuration of Segunda RFEF Group IV.

In this context, Sala's arrival is seen as a bid to add solidity and experience to a squad that is starting to take shape ahead of pre-season.

Pending the official announcement

While the club has not formally confirmed the signing, the information rests on solid, coinciding sources, which allows the deal to be considered practically done. With the usual nuances of transfer journalism — where official confirmation tends to arrive days after the news is already circulating with solid backing — everything indicates that David Sala will shortly be presented as a new Xerez CD player.

This newsroom will continue reporting as soon as the club officially announces the signing.
